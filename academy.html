Curriculum

THREE LEVELS OF MASTERY

🥉
Bronze Level

FOUNDATION

Grades 3 - 5

Focus: Fundamental skills, basic game understanding

  • Cradling
  • Passing
  • Catching
  • Ground balls
  • Basic positioning
Graduation: Complete all Bronze skill assessments
🥈
Silver Level

DEVELOPMENT

Grades 6 - 8

Focus: Advanced skills, team concepts, lacrosse IQ

  • Dodging
  • Shooting
  • Defensive footwork
  • Off-ball movement
  • Film study basics
Graduation: Complete all Silver assessments + demonstrate game application
🥇
Gold Level

ELITE

Grades 9 - 12

Focus: Elite performance, leadership, college preparation

  • Advanced systems
  • Position specialization
  • Film analysis
  • Mental preparation
Graduation: Complete Gold assessments + coaching recommendation
