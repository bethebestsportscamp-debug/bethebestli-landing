BOYS LACROSSE PROGRAMS

From beginner fundamentals to college-level preparation, we have a program designed for every stage of your lacrosse journey.

🌱

SPRING LEAGUE

Competitive spring league play with weekly skill development sessions for players of all levels.

Ages 8-18 $495/season
  • 10-week season with structured training plan
  • 8 games + 2 tournaments
  • Weekly practice included
  • Film review sessions
  • Position-specific coaching
Register
☀️

SUMMER CAMP

Intensive daily training camps throughout summer break focused on building fundamental skills.

Ages 6-16 $350/week
  • Half-day and full-day options available
  • Fundamentals focus for all skill levels
  • Guest college coaches throughout the summer
  • Equipment provided for beginners
  • Team-building activities and competitions
Register
🍂

FALL TRAVEL

High-level travel teams competing across the Northeast against top-tier competition.

Ages 10-18 $795/season
  • Weekend tournaments at premier venues
  • 3-4 travel events per season
  • Team uniforms included
  • College showcase opportunities
  • Video highlights for recruiting
Register
❄️

WINTER SKILLS

Indoor skill development sessions during the off-season to keep your game sharp year-round.

Ages 8-16 $275/8 weeks
  • Turf facility training in heated indoor space
  • Stick skills focus and development
  • Wall ball stations and technique work
  • Agility and conditioning programs
  • Small-sided games and scrimmages
Register
🥅

GOALIE ACADEMY

Specialized training for goalies at all levels with dedicated position-specific coaching.

Ages 10-18 $395/season
  • Position-specific coaching from former college goalies
  • Arc play development and positioning
  • Clearing techniques and outlet passing
  • Communication training and leadership
  • Mental preparation and game film study
Register
🎓

COLLEGE PREP

Recruiting guidance and showcase preparation to help you reach the next level of lacrosse.

Ages 14-18 $595/program
  • Video editing assistance for highlight reels
  • Coach outreach templates and strategy
  • NCAA rules education and compliance
  • Campus visit planning and coordination
  • Academic advising and eligibility guidance
