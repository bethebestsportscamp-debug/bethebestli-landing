What We Offer

Programs Built for Every Player

From first-timers to college-bound athletes, BTB has a path for every player.

Travel Teams

Compete at the highest level. Boys and girls teams from youth through high school competing in premier tournaments across the region.

Clinics

Position-specific and skills-based clinics run by our elite coaching staff. Sharpen your game in focused, high-rep environments.

Camps

Immersive multi-day camps designed to accelerate development. Full-day programming with game play, film, and position work.

Private Training

One-on-one and small group sessions tailored to your player's needs. Accelerated development on your schedule.

College Prep

Recruiting guidance, film packages, and showcase opportunities. We help families navigate the college recruiting process.

Tournaments

BTB hosts and competes in premier events including the BTB World Series. High-level competition all season long.
