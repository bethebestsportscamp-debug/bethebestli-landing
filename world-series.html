How It Works

TOURNAMENT FORMAT

01

Pool Play

Round-robin games to establish bracket seeding and ensure every team plays multiple competitive games.

02

Bracket Play

Single-elimination brackets based on pool play results. Every game matters. Win or go home.

03

Championship

Division finals on Sunday with MVP awards and trophies. Champions crowned in every division.
